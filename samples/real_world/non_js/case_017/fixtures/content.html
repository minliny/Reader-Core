Chapter 1: The Beginning

Once upon a time, in a land far away, there lived a young hero named Alex. Alex had always dreamed of going on an adventure, but never thought it would actually happen.

One day, a mysterious old man came to the village. He told Alex that the world was in danger, and only Alex could save it.

Alex was scared, but he knew he had to help. So he packed his bags and set off on his journey.
